About Mobile Song Scores

Mobile Song Scores displays public domain songs formatted for phone and projector use. There are no immediate plans to include copyrighted material. Please contact the copyright holders with such requests.

Contact us for any of the following reasons:

  • To report a copyright infringement
  • To request public domain song be added (no more than 5 at a time)
  • To request a correction
  • To request alternative lyrics, melodies, or harmonies
  • To become a contributor (using MuseScore)
  • To make suggestions or business proposals
